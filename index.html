Chenglong Wang (王成龙)

I am currently a postgraduate in Smart City and Big Data at School of Urban Planning and Design, Peking University, advised by Prof. Zhaoya Gong. I have a B.S. in Geographic Information Science from School of Resource and Environmental Sciences, Wuhan University. My research interests include Geospatial Artificial Intelligence, Spatial Analytics and Visual Understanding.
